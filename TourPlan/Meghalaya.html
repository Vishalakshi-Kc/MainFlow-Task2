Meghalaya Trip Plan

Day 1: Shillong - The Capital City

Morning:

  • Arrival in Shillong: Check into your hotel and freshen up.
  • Ward's Lake: Take a leisurely walk around this beautiful man-made lake.
  • Don Bosco Museum: Explore this museum to learn about the diverse cultures and traditions of Northeast India.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch at a local restaurant: Try some local Khasi cuisine.
  • Shillong Peak: Enjoy panoramic views of the city and surrounding hills.
  • Elephant Falls: Visit these picturesque falls located just outside Shillong.

Evening:

  • Police Bazar: Stroll through this bustling market area, perfect for shopping and trying local street food.
  • Dinner: Dine at a popular local restaurant.

Day 2: Cherrapunji - Land of Waterfalls

Morning:

  • Drive to Cherrapunji: Approximately 2-hour drive from Shillong.
  • Nohkalikai Falls: Visit one of the tallest waterfalls in India with a stunning viewpoint.

Afternoon:

  • Mawsmai Cave: Explore this easy-to-navigate limestone cave.
  • Seven Sisters Falls: Admire these majestic falls, especially during the monsoon season.
  • Thangkharang Park: Enjoy panoramic views of the plains of Bangladesh.

Evening:

  • Return to Shillong: Relax and unwind at your hotel.
  • Dinner: Enjoy a quiet dinner at your hotel or explore a new restaurant in Shillong.

Day 3: Mawlynnong and Dawki

Morning:

  • Drive to Mawlynnong: Known as the cleanest village in Asia.
  • Mawlynnong Village: Explore the village, visit the Living Root Bridge, and enjoy the scenic beauty.

Afternoon:

  • Drive to Dawki: Approximately 1.5-hour drive from Mawlynnong.
  • Umngot River: Take a boat ride on the crystal-clear waters of this river.
  • Lunch: Have a picnic by the river or try local cuisine at a nearby eatery.

Evening:

  • Drive back to Shillong: Approximately 3-hour drive.
  • Free Time: Spend your last evening exploring any remaining sights in Shillong or relaxing
  • Farewell Dinner: Enjoy a final meal at a local restaurant, savoring the flavors of Meghalaya
