Shimla Trip Plan

Day 1: Arrival and Local Sightseeing

Morning:

  • Arrival in Shimla: Check into your hotel and freshen up.
  • The Ridge and Mall Road: Start with a leisurely walk around the Ridge and explore the bustling Mall Road.

Afternoon:

  • Christ Church: Visit the iconic Christ Church on the Ridge.
  • Lunch: Enjoy lunch at one of the many cafes or restaurants on Mall Road.

Evening:

  • Jakhoo Temple: Take a short trek or drive to Jakhoo Temple, dedicated to Lord Hanuman, and enjoy panoramic views of Shimla.
  • Scandal Point: Visit this famous spot for a beautiful sunset view.
  • Dinner: Dine at a local restaurant, savoring Himachali cuisine.

Day 2: Excursion to Kufri and Mashobra

Morning:

  • Drive to Kufri: Approximately 1-hour drive from Shimla.
  • Kufri Fun World: Visit this amusement park with various rides and activities. Enjoy a horse ride to Mahasu Peak for stunning views.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch in Kufri: Have lunch at a local restaurant or cafe.
  • Mashobra: Drive to Mashobra and enjoy the natural beauty and tranquility. Visit the Craignano Nature Park and indulge in some adventure activities like zip-lining.

Evening:

  • Return to Shimla: Relax at your hotel.
  • Shopping: Explore Lakkar Bazaar, known for its wooden handicrafts and souvenirs.
  • Dinner: Enjoy dinner at a popular local restaurant.

Day 3: Heritage and Nature Walks

Morning:

  • Viceregal Lodge: Visit this historical building, also known as Rashtrapati Niwas, and take a guided tour to learn about its history and architecture.
  • Indian Institute of Advanced Study: Explore the beautiful gardens and museum.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Have lunch at a cafe or restaurant in the vicinity.
  • Glen and Annandale: Take a short trek to Glen, a lovely picnic spot, and visit Annandale, which houses an army museum and a golf course.

Evening:

  • Tara Devi Temple: Drive to Tara Devi Temple, located on a hilltop, and enjoy the serene surroundings and views.
  • Free Time: Spend your last evening exploring any remaining sights or relaxing.
  • Farewell Dinner: Enjoy a final meal at a recommended restaurant, savoring the local flavors.
