Alappuzha Trip Plan

Day 1: Arrival and Houseboat Experience

Morning:

  • Arrival in Alappuzha: Check into your hotel or directly board a houseboat.
  • Houseboat Cruise: Start your houseboat cruise on the backwaters. Enjoy the serene beauty of the palm-fringed waterways, paddy fields, and traditional village life.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Enjoy a freshly prepared traditional Kerala meal on the houseboat.
  • Cruise: Continue cruising through the backwaters, passing by small islands, lush greenery, and local life.

Evening:

  • Sunset: Experience a mesmerizing sunset from the deck of the houseboat.
  • Dinner: Have a delicious dinner on the houseboat.
  • Overnight Stay: Spend the night on the houseboat, anchored at a scenic spot.

Day 2: Alappuzha Beach and Local Attractions

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Enjoy breakfast on the houseboat
  • Disembark Houseboat: Check out from the houseboat and head to your hotel in Alappuzha.
  • Alappuzha Beach: Visit Alappuzha Beach, known for its picturesque views and historic pier.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Have lunch at a beachside restaurant.
  • St. Maryâ€™s Forane Church: Explore this historic church, known for its beautiful architecture.
  • Revi Karunakaran Museum: Visit this museum to see an impressive collection of antiques, artworks, and artifacts.

Evening:

  • Marari Beach: Head to Marari Beach, a serene and less crowded beach ideal for relaxation.
  • Dinner: Dine at a local restaurant, savoring traditional Kerala cuisine.

Day 3: Exploring the Backwaters and Village Life

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Enjoy breakfast at your hotel.
  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary: Take a short drive to the nearby Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary for bird watching and a nature walk.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Have lunch at a local restaurant in Kumarakom.
  • Backwater Village Tour: Take a guided tour of a traditional Kerala village to experience local culture, activities, and cuisine.

Evening:

  • Ambalapuzha Sree Krishna Temple: Visit this ancient temple known for its beautiful architecture and delicious payasam (sweet pudding).
  • Sunset Cruise: Enjoy a sunset cruise on Vembanad Lake, the longest lake in India.
